Module 4 · Part 2 · How It Works

From Demand Signal to Purchase Order

How the forecast number gets built, what alerts it triggers, and how one click becomes a PO.

01 · Inputs
📥
Where the numbers come from
Live data across 6 sources
  • Skio — subscription billing cycles & renewals
  • Shopify — historical sales & flavor mix
  • D365 — live inventory snapshots
  • BOM — RM & component requirements per SKU
  • Vendor config — lead times per SKU
  • Planning events — Loyalty, promos, campaigns
02 · Calculate
🧮
How the forecast is built
Subscription-aware demand model
  • 12-week / 90-day projection window
  • Per SKU × per warehouse breakdown
  • 3 billing-cycle subscription refill demand
  • Shopify one-time sales trend
  • Flavor mix + production balance
  • Event-adjusted impact (Loyalty, promos)
03 · Alert
🔔
What it tells us to do
Proactive, not reactive
  • Stockout warnings below runway threshold
  • RM runway — reorder before production gap
  • Per-warehouse runway in days
  • Flavor mix imbalance flags
  • Suggested reorder quantities per SKU
  • Slack alerts for critical items
04 · Procure
📋
How it becomes a PO
One click, fully wired
  • Click Create PO — quantities pre-filled from forecast
  • Auto-allocates across warehouses by demand
  • Generates Stord PO via API
  • Creates inbound shipment record
  • 2-way sync on RECEIVED_SHORT / OVER
  • D365 updates when stock lands

◆ 6 data sources in  →  forecast calculated  →  alerts fire  →  one click turns insight into a PO
